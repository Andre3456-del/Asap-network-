## What is DeFi

DeFi (Decentralized Finance) refers to financial services — lending, trading, borrowing —
built on blockchains without a central intermediary like a bank.

## What is an NFT

An NFT (Non-Fungible Token) is a unique, verifiable digital asset recorded on a blockchain,
commonly used for art, collectibles, and access passes.
